# Quantum computing, problem first

Most quantum computing explanations begin with a qubit. This blog usually does not. We start with the problem, earn the theory, and keep everything runnable.

---

## Series

**Linear Algebra for Fun and Profit** — the linear algebra behind quantum computing and machine learning. Each post builds from first principles with a single running example you can check by hand.

**The Quantum Bottleneck** *(coming soon)* — eight industry problems, from delivery routes to carbon capture, each paired with the quantum algorithm that could solve it. Companion to the upcoming book, with runnable notebooks and [Circuit Bench](../circuit-bench/index.md) side paths.

**[From Saturday to Co-Author](https://johnazariah.github.io/tags/from-saturday-to-coauthor/)** — a ten-part series on the [main blog](https://johnazariah.github.io) documenting the journey from learning QAOA on a Saturday morning to co-authoring a quantum computing paper.
